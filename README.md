# Netflix-Evolution-Analysis
# Netflix Evolution Analysis (2008–2021)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Netflix content trends from 2008 to 2021 using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).

The goal is to understand growth patterns, content strategy, rating trends, and duration behavior.

---

## Business Questions Answered

1. Which year had the maximum content growth?
2. How do Movies and TV Shows growth patterns differ?
3. What is the trend of Indian content on Netflix?
4. How have content ratings changed over time?
5. Are movies getting shorter over the years?
6. How quickly does Netflix add newly released content?

---

## Key Insights

- Netflix experienced peak growth during its expansion phase after 2016.
- TV Shows increased significantly compared to earlier years.
- Indian content saw rapid growth in recent years.
- Mature ratings such as TV-MA became more dominant.
- Most content is added within 0–2 years of release.
